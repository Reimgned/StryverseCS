    Common Questions

    Is this a game or a streaming service?

    It's the missing link between them. Streaming is passive; games require skill and time. Stryverse is a Massively Multiplayer Narrative. It requires no controller skills‚Äîjust your opinion.

    Do I need to watch hours of content?

    No. We respect your time. Episodes are designed to be 3-5 minutes long. High impact, zero fluff. It fits perfectly into your morning coffee ritual or evening commute.

    Why join the Alpha instead of waiting for public launch?

    The "Genesis Vote" happens only once. The decisions made by Alpha members will set the permanent canon/lore for the entire platform's history. You can watch history later, or you can write it now.

    What happens if I miss a vote?

    The world moves on without you. That is the point. Unlike a video game save file, the Stryverse timeline is persistent. Real stakes mean the clock doesn't stop.

    I'm a writer/animator. Can I build here?

    We are hand-picking the first wave of "Universe Architects." If you want to build a story that talks back to you, join the waitlist. We will be sending out Creator Applications to founding members first.

    Is there a cost?

    The Genesis Era is completely free. We are building a community of founders, not just subscribers. Get in before the gates close.
