    It's the missing link between them. Streaming is passive; games require skill and time. Stryverse is a Massively Multiviewer Narrative. It requires no controller skills‚Äîjust your opinion.

    Do I need to watch hours of content?

    No. You do you boo. Besides, most episodes are designed to be 3-5 minutes long.

    Why join the Alpha instead of waiting for public launch?

    The "Genesis Vote" happens only once. The decisions made by Alpha members will set the canon/lore for the platform's launch. You can watch history later, or you can write it now.

    What happens if I miss a vote?

    The world moves on without you. That is the point. Unlike a video game save file, the Stryverse timeline is persistent. Real stakes mean the clock doesn't stop.

    I'm a writer/animator. Can I build here?

    If you want to build a story that talks back to you, join the waitlist. In platform tools are in the works.

    Is there a cost?

    The Genesis Era is completely free. We are building a community of founders, not just subscribers. Get in before the gates close.
